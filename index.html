Welcome to Dudhwa National Park

Welcome to Dudhwa National Park, a pristine wilderness nestled in the Terai belt of Uttar Pradesh along the Indo-Nepal border. Spanning 490 square kilometers of lush sal forests, grasslands, and wetlands, Dudhwa offers an unparalleled wildlife experience for nature lovers, families, and adventure seekers alike.

Home to Bengal tigers, Indian rhinos, Asian elephants, and over 450 bird species, this UNESCO-recognized ecosystem represents one of India's most successful conservation stories.

Whether you're seeking thrilling jungle safaris, peaceful birdwatching, or simply a retreat into nature, Dudhwa promises an unforgettable journey.
