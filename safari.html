Choose Your Safari Experience

Dudhwa offers multiple safari options to suit different preferences and adventure levels. Each type provides unique perspectives on the park’s incredible wildlife.

🚙 Jeep Safari (Most Popular)

Duration: 3–4 hours

Capacity: Maximum 6 persons per jeep

Description: Explore Dudhwa’s core zones in specially designed 4×4 open safari vehicles. Experienced drivers and trained naturalist guides accompany every safari, sharing insights about wildlife behavior, tracks, and forest ecology.

Pricing (Approximate):

  • Indian Nationals: ₹1,500–2,500 per jeep + entry fees
  • Foreign Nationals: ₹3,000–4,000 per jeep + entry fees
  • Guide charges: Included

Best For: Families, first-time visitors, wildlife photography

🐘 Elephant Safari

Duration: 1.5–2 hours

Availability: Morning slot only (7:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

Zones: Sonaripur, Kishanpur

Description: Experience the forest from a unique vantage point atop an elephant. This traditional safari method allows closer approach to wildlife without disturbing them. Particularly effective for rhino viewing in the Sonaripur zone.

Pricing (Approximate):

  • Indian Nationals: ₹600 per person
  • Foreign Nationals: ₹1,200 per person
  • Mahout charges: Included

Best For: Rhino viewing, traditional safari experience, close wildlife encounters

Safari Booking Tips

  1. Book in Advance: Safaris fill quickly during peak season (December–January). Book 7–15 days ahead.
  2. Morning vs Evening: Morning safaris offer better sightings and cooler temperatures.
  3. Choose Multiple Zones: Plan safaris across 2–3 zones for comprehensive wildlife viewing.
  4. Private vs Shared: Private jeeps offer flexibility; shared safaris are budget-friendly.
  5. Weather Considerations: Overcast days can extend animal activity beyond morning hours.
