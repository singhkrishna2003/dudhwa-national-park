Where to Stay Near Dudhwa

From luxury resorts to budget-friendly lodges and eco-homestays, Dudhwa offers accommodation options for every traveler.

Dudhwa offers diverse accommodation options ranging from luxury resorts to budget-friendly forest lodges. Most properties are located near Palia Kalan, around 5–8 km from the park entrance.

Luxury Resorts

Jaagir Manor ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Location: Palia Kalan (7 km from park)

Features:

  • Heritage property with colonial architecture
  • Spacious suites with modern amenities
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant
  • Swimming pool and spa
  • Beautifully landscaped gardens
  • Personalized safari arrangements

Price Range: ₹8,000–15,000 per night

Best For: Luxury travelers, honeymooners, international guests

Mid-Range Resorts

Dudhwa Jungle Lore Resort

Location: 6 km from Dudhwa entrance

Features:

  • Comfortable cottages in forest setting
  • Restaurant with Indian & Continental cuisine
  • Guided nature walks
  • Bonfire arrangements
  • Safari booking assistance

Price Range: ₹3,500–6,000 per night

Best For: Families, wildlife enthusiasts, value seekers

Tiger Den Club & Resort

Location: Palia Kalan (5.9 km from park)

Features:

  • Spacious rooms with garden views
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant
  • Green surroundings
  • Budget-friendly packages

Price Range: ₹3,000–5,000 per night

Best For: Budget-conscious families, group travelers

Dudhwa Jungle Hideaway Resort

Location: In the lap of nature, Palia Kalan

Features:

  • Cottages and standard rooms
  • Nature-inspired décor
  • Restaurant with local & international menu
  • Children’s play area
  • Safari coordination services

Price Range: ₹2,500–4,500 per night

Best For: Nature lovers, families with children

Clarks Inn Dudhwa

Location: Palia Kalan

Features:

  • Comfortable rooms with essential amenities
  • Restaurant serving Indian cuisine
  • Budget-friendly tariffs
  • Safari arrangements available

Price Range: ₹2,000–3,500 per night

Best For: Budget travelers, solo visitors

Homestays and Eco-Camps

Local Homestays

Several villages near Dudhwa offer authentic homestay experiences where visitors can immerse themselves in local Tharu culture, enjoy traditional cuisine, and support community-based tourism.

Price Range: ₹1,000–2,000 per night (including meals)

Best For: Cultural immersion, responsible tourism, budget travelers

Booking Tips

  1. Advance Reservation: Book 15–30 days ahead during peak season (November–February)
  2. Package Deals: Safari-inclusive packages are often more economical
  3. Check Inclusions: Confirm meals, safaris, and transfers
  4. Location Matters: Stay within 10 km of the park entrance
  5. Contact Directly: Reach out to resorts for best rates and customized packages
