Getting to Dudhwa National Park

Dudhwa National Park is well-connected to major cities across North India. The nearest town is Palia Kalan, which serves as the base for most visitors.

By Air

Nearest Airport: Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport, Lucknow (221 km)

Flight Connectivity: Daily flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Patna, and other major cities via Air India, IndiGo, SpiceJet, and Vistara.

From Airport to Dudhwa:

  • Taxi: 4–5 hours drive (approximately ₹4,000–6,000)
  • Pre-booked transfers through your resort
  • Self-drive rental cars available

Alternative: Pantnagar Airport (160 km) has limited connectivity but is closer to the park.

By Train

Nearest Railway Stations:

  1. Dudhwa Railway Station (12 km)
    • Small station with limited trains
    • Suitable for connecting from nearby towns
  2. Mailani Railway Station (35 km)
    • Gateway for Kishanpur Wildlife Sanctuary
    • Better connectivity to major cities
    • Regular trains from Delhi, Lucknow, Bareilly

From Railway Station: Taxis and auto-rickshaws are available. It is recommended to pre-arrange resort pickup.

By Road

From Delhi

450 km • 8–9 hours

Route: NH-9 via Moradabad, Bareilly, Lakhimpur Kheri

Regular buses available; best to drive or hire a taxi

From Lucknow

220 km • 4–5 hours

Route via Sitapur, Lakhimpur Kheri

State transport buses available; well-maintained roads

From Nainital

180 km • 4–5 hours

Scenic drive through the Terai region

Ideal for combining hill station and wildlife experience

From Bareilly

125 km • 3 hours

Shortest route from a major city with good road conditions

Bus Services: UPSRTC operates regular buses. Private luxury coaches are available from Delhi and Lucknow. Resorts can arrange private transfers on request.

Local Transport

  • Within Park: Only authorized safari vehicles allowed
  • Palia Kalan to Park: Safari vehicles and taxis available
  • Between Zones: Arrange transport through your accommodation
